Domains

Domain: Web Development
  • Design and implement a responsive e-commerce website with user-friendly navigation and checkout process.
  • Develop a web-based project management tool that enables teams to collaborate and track progress efficiently.
  • Create a web application that connects mentors with mentees based on their skills and interests.
  • Build a social media platform with interactive features such as real-time messaging and user profiles.
  • Develop a web-based food delivery platform that allows users to order from multiple restaurants and track their deliveries.
Domain: Mobile App Development
  • Design and build a fitness tracking app that monitors and records users' physical activities and health metrics.
  • Develop a mobile app for language learning that incorporates gamification and personalized learning paths.
  • Create a mobile app for event management that enables users to create, manage, and promote events.
  • Build a mobile app for peer-to-peer ride-sharing that ensures secure and convenient transportation options.
  • Develop a mobile app for personal finance management that tracks expenses, sets budgets, and provides financial insights.
Domain: Data Science
  • Build a data visualization tool that allows users to explore and analyze complex datasets with interactive visualizations.
  • Develop a machine learning model for predicting stock market trends based on historical data and market indicators.
  • Create a recommendation system that suggests personalized movie or music recommendations to users.
  • Build a sentiment analysis tool that accurately classifies social media posts or customer reviews as positive, negative, or neutral.
  • Develop a fraud detection system that uses machine learning algorithms to identify suspicious patterns and transactions.
Domain: Cybersecurity
  • Develop a secure password management mobile app that utilizes encryption and multi-factor authentication.
  • Build a network intrusion detection system that can detect and prevent cyber attacks and unauthorized access.
  • Create a secure file transfer protocol (FTP) application that encrypts data during transmission and storage.
  • Develop a phishing awareness and training platform that educates users about common phishing techniques and how to identify them.
  • Build a vulnerability scanning tool that identifies security loopholes in web applications and provides recommendations for mitigation.
Domain: Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Develop an AI-powered virtual assistant that can perform tasks such as scheduling appointments, setting reminders, and answering questions.
  • Build an AI-based recommendation system that suggests personalized products or services to users based on their preferences and behavior.
  • Create an AI model for natural language processing that can accurately analyze and understand text data.
  • Develop an AI-powered image recognition system that can classify and tag images based on their content.
  • Build an AI-based chatbot for customer support that can understand and respond to user queries effectively.

Rules

  • Participants must bring their own systems for the hackathon. The organizing team will only provide internet connectivity. It is the responsibility of each team to ensure they have the necessary hardware and software tools to participate effectively.

  • Each team should choose a designated color to represent their identity. All team members are required to wear the chosen color during the event for easy identification

  • Throughout the entire duration of the event, a minimum of 50 percent of the team members must be present at the designated arena. Failure to meet this requirement may result in disqualification

  • Each team must consist of 4 to 6 members, ensuring a collaborative environment. It is mandatory to have at least one female participant in each team, promoting diversity and inclusivity

  • Participants are required to remain on the premises for the entire duration of the 48-hour hackathon. Leaving the premises during the event is strictly prohibited

  • Any participant found engaging in unethical practices, including but not limited to cheating, plagiarism, or unauthorized use of resources, will lead to disqualification of the entire team. Upholding integrity and ethical conduct is paramount in maintaining a fair and competitive environment

    • NOTE: These rules and regulations are in place to ensure a fair, inclusive, and professional hackathon experience for all participants. Violation of any of these rules may result in penalties or disqualification, as determined by the organizing committee.
